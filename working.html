Set-up Election

On successful registration, with our effortless ballot setup, you can set-up election in no time. This process involves 3 steps:
ADD ELECTION DETAILS: Add election details like election date and time, candidate’s names and photos.
ADD VOTER LIST: Drag and drop to import the list of voters from our convenient spreadsheet template.
REVIEW & LAUNCH: Once you've entered all the required details, review it and then you are good to go. Schedule a start/end date or immediately launch the election.
